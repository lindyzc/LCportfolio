    • Lindy Chiu (趙瑩瑩) is a Chinese-American interdisciplinary artist born and raised in New York City. After completing her dual BA in Studio Art: Printmaking & Biological Sciences from SUNY Binghamton, she balances work with non-profit art institutions while maintaining an active art practice.


    Her upbringing informs her practices by serving as a lens to look through. Through film photography, she captures moments of solitude and sentimentality, while her ceramics practice allows her to explore creating clay artifacts of time, memory, and emotion.
